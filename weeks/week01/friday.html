✨ Did you know?
🖊️ Diagrams are half the marks in Science!
In ICSE exams, well-labelled diagrams earn full marks. Every Friday we practise — neat, accurate, in pencil!

How to draw a good diagram

  • Use a pencil — never pen
  • Draw with a single clean line
  • Label clearly with a ruler
  • Write the title below
  • Keep it large enough to read

✨ Did you know?
📜 Kannada has a 2000-year literary history!
The oldest Kannada inscription dates to 450 CE. Great works like Kavirajamarga were written over 1,000 years ago.

Kannada Grammar basics

  • 3 genders: masculine, feminine, neuter
  • Nouns change form based on use (cases)
  • Verbs agree with subject in gender and number
  • Sentence order: Subject → Object → Verb
