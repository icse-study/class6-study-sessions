🌍 The Earth — Latitudes & Longitudes

Latitude lines run east–west, measure N/S from Equator (0°).

Longitude lines run north–south, measure E/W from Prime Meridian (0°).

Location = Latitude° N/S, Longitude° E/W

Key latitudes: Equator 0°, Tropic of Cancer 23.5°N, Tropic of Capricorn 23.5°S, Arctic Circle 66.5°N

🏛️ The Indus Valley Civilisation

One of the world's oldest civilisations (3300–1300 BCE), found along the Indus River.

  • Major cities: Mohenjo-daro and Harappa
  • Known for advanced town planning, drainage systems, and trade
  • Declined around 1900 BCE (reasons debated — floods, drought, migration)
3300 BCE → 2600 BCE (peak) → 1900 BCE (decline)
