📖 Literature — Siddharoodha Fair (Monday)

Key ideas

Fairs (ಜಾತ್ರೆಗಳು) are socio-economic unifiers — livelihood to artisans, culture for children, communal harmony. The Hubballi (ಹುಬ್ಬಳ್ಳಿ) Siddharoodha Math was active in the freedom struggle, linked to Gandhi & Tilak.

ಅಂಗಾರ=spark · ಬಂಗಾರ=gold · ಜೋಳಿಗೆ=sack · ಹೋಳಿಗೆ=sweet · ಜನಸ್ಥೋಮ=huge crowd · ಹಿರೇಮಣಿ=leader · ಕರ್ಮಭೂಮಿ=land of duty
💡 Both slogans elevate the seer to a national benefactor — he gives more than he takes.
🗣️ Grammar (Friday)

Tenses · Gender–Number · Sandhi

ಕಾಲ: ವರ್ತಮಾನ (-ಉತ್ತೇನೆ/-ಉತ್ತಾಳೆ) · ಭೂತ (-ದನು/-ದಳು/-ಇತು) · ಭವಿಷ್ಯ (-ವನು/-ವಳು). Verb agrees with subject's gender & number.
ಲಿಂಗ: ಪುಲ್ಲಿಂಗ / ಸ್ತ್ರೀಲಿಂಗ / ನಪುಂಸಕಲಿಂಗ. ವಚನ: ಏಕ → ಬಹು by -ಗಳು / -ಅರು.
ಸಂಧಿ: ಆಗಮ (add) · ಲೋಪ (drop) · ಆದೇಶ (replace).

🧠 Did You Know?
A century-long dream realised
Architect Antoni Gaudí said of his unfinished Sagrada Família, "My client is not in a hurry" (meaning God). On 10 June 2026, exactly 100 years after Gaudí's death, the Tower of Jesus Christ (172.5 m) was blessed — making the Sagrada Família the tallest Catholic church in the world.
