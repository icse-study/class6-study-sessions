✨ Did you know?
🖊️ Diagrams are half the marks in Science!
In ICSE exams, well-labelled diagrams can earn you full marks. Every Friday we practise drawing and labelling — neat, accurate, and in pencil!

How to draw a good science diagram

  • Use a pencil — never pen
  • Draw with a single, clean line
  • Label clearly with a ruler and horizontal lines
  • Write the title below the diagram
  • Keep it large enough to read

✨ Did you know?
📜 Kannada has a 2000-year literary history!
The oldest known Kannada inscription dates back to 450 CE. Great works like Kavirajamarga were written in Kannada over 1,000 years ago.

Kannada Grammar basics

  • Kannada has 3 genders: masculine, feminine, neuter
  • Nouns change form based on how they are used (cases)
  • Verbs agree with the subject in gender and number
  • Sentences usually follow Subject → Object → Verb order

✨ Did you know?
🌱 You share 60% of your DNA with a banana!
All living things share some DNA because we all evolved from common ancestors. Humans share 99% of DNA with chimpanzees and 85% with mice!
