Silent end consonants

d, g, m, n, p, q, s, t, x, z are usually silent at the end of a word.

Silent: froid (cold) · beaucoup (many) · trois (three) · prix (price)
💡 Exceptions — usually PRONOUNCED at the end: b, c, r, f, l (club, avec, avril). Memory hook: "CaReFuL".

Oral vs Nasal

Oral vowels come out through the mouth only (a in salle). Nasal vowels come out through the mouth AND nose, and are followed by n or m (an in blanc).

🇫🇷 French Culture — Key Facts

About France

Capital: Paris  ·  National Day: 14 July (le 14 juillet)
Anthem: La Marseillaise  ·  Flag: Tricolore — blue, white, red (bleu, blanc, rouge)
Currency: Euro (earlier: the Franc)  ·  Famous cheese: Camembert
Famous rivers: the Seine and the Loire
Nickname: "L'Hexagone" — because France's shape has six sides.

French Punctuation (la ponctuation)

@ — l'arobase  ·  « » — les guillemets
! — le point d'exclamation  ·  , — la virgule  ·  . — le point

✍️ Writing Practice — answer on paper, then check
16. Explain the difference between oral vowels and nasal vowels. Give one example of each.
▸ Show model answer
Oral vowels are produced only through the mouth (e.g. a in "salle"). Nasal vowels are produced through the mouth and nose, and are followed by n/m (e.g. an in "blanc").
17. Why is France sometimes called "L'Hexagone"?
▸ Show model answer
Because the outline (shape) of France roughly resembles a six-sided hexagon.
18. What is the rule for pronouncing consonants at the end of French words? List the silent ones.
▸ Show model answer
Usually d, g, m, n, p, q, s, t, x, z are silent at the end. The exceptions, usually pronounced, are b, c, r, f, l.
19. How is the French 'r' different from the English 'r'? Describe it and give an example.
▸ Show model answer
The French 'r' is a throat (guttural) sound made at the back of the throat, like a soft gargle — not rolled with the tongue tip. Example: "frère" (frehr).
20. Write the French words for: (a) the comma, (b) the exclamation mark, (c) the quotation marks.
▸ Show model answer
(a) la virgule   (b) le point d'exclamation   (c) les guillemets
