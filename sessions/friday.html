🧬 The Cell — Basic Unit of Life

Every living thing is made of cells. Key parts:

  • Nucleus — control centre, contains DNA
  • Cell membrane — controls what enters/exits
  • Mitochondria — powerhouse, makes energy
  • Chloroplast — only in plant cells, for photosynthesis
6CO₂ + 6H₂O + light → C₆H₁₂O₆ + 6O₂

🗣️ Kannada — Common Words & Grammar

Common words:

  • ನೀರು (Nīru) — Water  |  ಹಾಲು (Hālu) — Milk
  • ಮನೆ (Mane) — House  |  ಶಾಲೆ (Shāle) — School
  • ಅಮ್ಮ (Amma) — Mother  |  ಅಪ್ಪ (Appa) — Father

Plurals: Add ಗಳು (-gaḷu) to most nouns.

ಮರ (tree) → ಮರಗಳು (trees)
